# FIVE OAKS BUSINESSES DATABASE

This file is a canon reference for businesses, employers, commercial properties, and working enterprises in the Five Oaks saga.

## Five Oaks Ranch

**Type:** Cattle ranch and family enterprise.

**Owners:** Jake Kincaid, Krys Callahan Kincaid, Matt Haskins, Luke Rawlins, and Jace Callahan hold equal shares.

**Primary Work:**
- Cattle.
- Horses.
- Hogs.
- Chickens.
- Corn and feed production.
- Livestock buying, breeding, moving, and sales.

**Management:**
- The founders make major decisions together.
- Matt oversees planning, money, and structure.
- Jake handles enforcement and difficult physical work.
- Luke manages food, morale, and many daily practical needs.
- Jace handles pressure and direct confrontation.
- Krys manages books, banking, negotiation, and outside perception.
- Robert later serves as ranch foreman.

**Importance:** Five Oaks grows from survival into a multigenerational family enterprise.

---

## Callahan Farm

**Type:** Family farm.

**Owners:** Roger and Marie Callahan.

**Importance:** Childhood workplace and expected inheritance of the Callahan children. Roger's control and criticism drive several children away.

---

## Johnson Farm

**Type:** Farm employer.

**Owner:** Albert "Old Man" Johnson.

**Workers:** Jace, Jake, Matt, Luke, and Duncan during the founders' early years.

**Reputation:** Poor pay, relentless labor, little respect.

**Importance:** Johnson's treatment helps convince the founders to seek independence.

---

## Milliron Farm

**Type:** Farm employer.

**Owners:** Henry and Martha Milliron.

**Workers:** Jake, Matt, Luke, and Krys after they leave Johnson's farm.

**Reputation:** Honest work, fair treatment, food, shelter, and respect.

**Importance:** Gives the founders proof that work does not have to come with humiliation.

---

## High Willow Ranch

**Type:** Large ranch.

**Owner:** Silas Vance.

**Known Employees and Agents:**
- Amos Bennett.
- Coy Walker.
- Dylan Kennedy.

**Business Method:** Uses wealth, hired agents, pressure, threats, and influence to gain valuable land.

**Importance:** Primary commercial rival during the founders' land conflict.

---

## Sawyer and Dawson's Oil Venture

**Type:** Oil exploration and drilling enterprise.

**Founders:** Sawyer Callahan and Dawson Callahan.

**Location:** Patterson property.

**Funding and Support:** Requires land access and financial backing from the Five Oaks founders.

**Labor:** Includes Fred Ward and other hired rig workers.

**Importance:** Represents the next generation building something new rather than merely inheriting Five Oaks.

---

## Patterson Oil Well

**Type:** Active drilling operation.

**Operators:** Sawyer and Dawson Callahan.

**Location:** Patterson property.

**Known Features:**
- Drilling rig.
- Worker housing in repaired outbuildings.
- Equipment, supply, and transport needs.

**Importance:** Central business setting of Sawyer and Dawson's story.

---

## Patel's Eatery

**Type:** Restaurant.

**Owner:** Patel.

**Location:** Red Willow Crossing.

**Known For:**
- Roast chicken.
- Mashed potatoes.
- Corn.
- Coffee.
- Familiar, dependable meals.

**Customers:** Multiple generations of the Five Oaks family and ranch hands.

**Importance:** A neutral meeting place where business, family, and town life overlap.

---

## The Belle Époque

**Type:** French restaurant.

**Location:** Red Willow Crossing.

**Known For:** Formal meals and a more refined dining experience than Patel's.

**Importance:** Site of family dinners, pregnancy news, and the first major shared meal between the Kincaid and Echevarría families.

---

## Bank of Red Willow Crossing

**Type:** Bank.

**Location:** Red Willow Crossing.

**Known Connections:**
- Krys works with banking and ledgers.
- The bank plays a role in the founders' early outlaw period.
- Emery is associated with the bank in later years.
- Letters, orders, money, investments, and legal arrangements pass through it.

**Importance:** Financial center for the town and Five Oaks.

---

## Feed Store

**Type:** Agricultural supply business.

**Location:** Red Willow Crossing.

**Services:** Feed and ranch supplies.

**Importance:** Longstanding Five Oaks supplier and source of town information.

## Livery

**Type:** Horse boarding, rental, and transport business.

## Conrad Hensley's Shipping Enterprise

**Type:** Shipping and logistics business.

**Owner:** Conrad Hensley.

**Work:** Connects lumber, rail freight, suppliers, and expanding markets.

**Known Assistance:** Krys helps him close deals by recognizing practical solutions and gathering freight information.

---
