# FIVE OAKS TIMELINE DATABASE

This file is a canon reference for major dates and chronological anchors in the Five Oaks saga. Use exact dates where established. Where only an approximate year is known, the entry is marked approximate.

## 1865

- January 9: Jace Michael Callahan is born.
- March 17: Matthew Alan Haskins is born.

## 1866

- November 3: Jacob Weston Kincaid is born.

## 1868

- August 11: Lucas Paul Rawlins is born.

## 1869

- April 22: Krysten Irene Callahan is born.

## 1877–1879 Approximate

- Matt spends childhood being passed from home to home while his mother struggles with laudanum addiction.
- Jake grows up beneath Victor's abuse and the cruelty of Curtis, Dwayne, and Albert.
- Luke protects Astrid and Iris from their violent alcoholic father.

## 1877

- Approximate: Matt, age twelve, finds his mother dead from a laudanum overdose and begins surviving on his own.

## 1879

- Approximate: Jake, age thirteen, leaves Victor's household and chooses hunger over remaining there.

## 1881

- The prequel era begins.
- Krys, age seventeen, returns from finishing school in New York.
- Jace is twenty.
- Rance is fifteen.
- Royce is thirteen.
- Rhys is eleven.
- Jake, Matt, Luke, and Jace work under Albert "Old Man" Johnson.
- The future founders' relationships begin taking shape.
- Luke is around thirteen to fourteen during the period when he leaves his abusive childhood home.

## 1881–1883 Approximate

- Jake, Matt, Luke, and Krys work for Henry and Martha Milliron after leaving Johnson's farm.
- Ezra Pennington pressures the Millirons for access across their land.
- The founders begin moving toward independence.

## 1884–1885

- The bank and freight-job era of Brothers by the Gun.
- Krys works inside the bank and helps open the rear exit during the robbery.
- Luke is wounded with a knife and develops a fever.
- Matt handles planning.
- Jake remains outside during the bank operation.
- Jace is absent during the bank robbery.

## 1887–1888

- Roger and Marie visit the ranch for roughly a month.
- Roger criticizes the equal ownership of Five Oaks and the communal household structure.
- Roger strikes Rhys for the first time.
- Jace later steps in when Roger appears ready to strike Rhys again.
- Roger objects to Krys touching Matt during Matt's episodes.
- Roger objects to the name Kai.
- Krys meets the traveling Indigenous man Kai and receives permission to use his name for her child.

## 1888

- Kai Weston Kincaid is born.
- Rance's son Ethan is born around the same time and is the same age as Kai.

## 1890 Approximate

- Waya Red Hawk is born.

## 1892 Approximate

- Wesley Jon Kincaid is born.
- Samantha "Sami" Rawlins is born around the same general period.

## 1897

- Sawyer Michael Callahan is born.
- Dawson James Callahan is born.
- Sawyer and Dawson are paternal twins.

## 1898

- Jennifer Marie Callahan is born.

## 1906

- Tsula Sixkill is born.

## Early 1900s Approximate

- Eli Callahan grows up at Five Oaks.
- Cole, Sawyer, Dawson, Jennifer, and later Caleb grow up as the next generation.
- Sawyer and Dawson become fascinated with oil, drilling, and the possibility of what lies beneath the land.

## Around 1910

- Eli, age fifteen, is recruited by Jesse "Whisper" Winston after Whisper exploits his anger toward Jace.
- Eli enters outlaw life and remains away from home for more than five years.

## Around 1910–1911

- Waya, about twenty, becomes guardian to eight-year-old Tsula after Awinita "Fawn" Red Hawk and Kanuna Sixkiller die within months of one another.

## 1916

- Founders' locked ages:
  - Krys: 47.
  - Jake: 50.
  - Matt: 51.
  - Jace: 50.
  - Susanna: 48.
- Next generation locked ages:
  - Kai: 27.
  - Wes: 23.
  - Sami: 23.
  - Eli: 21.
  - Cole: 20.
  - Sawyer and Dawson: 19.
  - Jennifer: 18.
  - Caleb: approximately 3–4.
  - Emily: approximately 2–3.
- Eli leaves Ironjaw Winston's hideout and rides back toward Missouri.
- Eli watches Five Oaks from the ridge before returning.
- Whisper follows and threatens Eli's family.
- Krys pushes Eli aside and is shot by Whisper.
- Eli kills Whisper moments later.
- Krys undergoes two surgeries and remains unconscious for ten days.
- Eli remains near the family instead of leaving again.
- Matt decides Jennifer will inherit his full share of Five Oaks.
- Eli receives a cabin, giving him a permanent place at the ranch.
- Cole meets Tate Hudson.
- Kai meets Paloma Echevarría.
- Kai and Paloma marry at Five Oaks.
- Wes and Sami reveal that Sami is expecting Wesley Lucas Kincaid.

## 1917

- Sawyer and Dawson seek land and financial backing for their oil venture.
- The founders debate whether to support the twins.
- Krys supports the plan immediately.
- Jake is reluctant but ultimately stands with Krys.
- Luke believes the boys deserve a chance.
- Matt worries about the money and is persuaded after Tate shows unexpected knowledge.
- Jace opposes the plan but is outvoted.
- Oil work begins on the Patterson property.
- Fred Ward, age forty-five, is hired to work on the rig.
- Fred brings sons David, seventeen; Micah, fourteen; and Colin, eleven.
- The Ward family lives in a repaired outbuilding on the Patterson property.
- Micah flees Fred's abuse and hides in the Five Oaks loft for several days.
- Krys finds Micah, cleans his injuries, feeds him, and shelters him on the couch in her and Jake's cabin because Cole and Tate still occupy the loft.
- Jake, Matt, and Luke each recognize pieces of their own childhood in Micah.
- Fred eventually leaves with David and Colin but abandons Micah at Five Oaks.
- Krys comes out to Micah after the truck drives away, making clear that he still has a home.

## 1918

- Kai and Paloma build their life in a cabin on the north path.
- Wes and Sami live in their cabin behind the main house.
- Cole and Tate's story centers on family acceptance, secrecy, and choosing an honest life.
- Jennifer and Waya's relationship develops across Five Oaks, the Patterson side of the property, the creek, and Waya's community.
- Jennifer marries Waya Red Hawk.
- Tsula becomes Jennifer's stepson in practice and in family life.
- By the end of Jennifer and Waya's story, Jace fully accepts Tate.
- Sawyer and Dawson also accept Tate and speak to him normally as family.

## 1920

- Black Gold Under Dust centers on Sawyer and Dawson and the Patterson oil field.
- The oil venture becomes a defining part of the next generation's future.

## Later Years

- Marie marries Bob Masters years after Roger's death.
- Royce spends twenty-five years in prison before returning to the world and facing whether he still belongs.
- Kai and Paloma eventually have their first child by the end of the final book. Kai is nearly thirty-two and Paloma is roughly ten years younger.
- Krys dies in the epilogue at age sixty-nine.
- Marie dies after Royce and Rance leave.

## Inheritance Plan

- Krys's share of Five Oaks goes to Kai.
- Jake's share goes to Wes.
- Luke's share goes to Sami.
- Matt's full share goes to Jennifer.
- Jace's share is divided among Eli, Cole, Sawyer, Dawson, and Caleb.
- Emily is not included in Jace's share.
