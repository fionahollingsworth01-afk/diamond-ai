Mateo Echevarría 
Role: Husband • Father • Rancher • Head of the Echevarría family
Family:
Husband of Catalina Echevarría.
Father of Paloma and Isabella Echevarría.
Father-in-law of Kai Kincaid.
Core Spine:
Mateo believes a father's first duty is protecting his family. Love must be proven through responsibility, sacrifice, and unwavering commitment. He measures a man's character by how he treats the people entrusted to him, not by what he says.
Background Foundation:
Raised with strong Spanish traditions where family honor, hard work, and personal responsibility carried tremendous importance. Built a life through persistence and expects the same determination from others.
Core Formation:
Family comes before comfort.
Respect is earned.
Promises must be kept.
A father never stops protecting his children.
Core Beliefs:
Character matters more than wealth.
Marriage is a lifelong commitment.
A man provides before he speaks.
Trust is earned through actions.
Greatest Fear:
Entrusting one of his daughters to a man who cannot protect or cherish her.
Greatest Strength:
Steadfast devotion, discipline, wisdom, and fierce protection of his family.
Fatal Flaw:
His protective nature can become intimidating and overly cautious, making it difficult for others to prove themselves.
Psychological Markers:
Observes before judging.
Speaks carefully.
Rarely wastes words.
Dry sense of humor that often arrives unintentionally.
Softens considerably once trust has been earned.
Relationship With Kai:
Initially studies Kai with deep skepticism, believing no man is automatically worthy of Paloma. As Kai consistently proves his character, Mateo gradually accepts him not merely as a son-in-law but as another son.
Locked Thematic Core:
Protection born from responsibility.
Catalina Echevarría 
Role: Wife • Mother • Heart of the Echevarría family
Family:
Wife of Mateo Echevarría.
Mother of Paloma and Isabella Echevarría.
Mother-in-law of Kai Kincaid.
Core Spine:
Catalina believes love is expressed through quiet care, patience, and keeping a family together. She steadies strong personalities without competing with them and reminds everyone that kindness is never weakness.
Background Foundation:
Raised in a close Spanish family where traditions, faith, hospitality, and family unity shaped everyday life.
Core Formation:
Gentleness strengthens families.
Traditions preserve identity.
Love is shown through daily acts of care.
Peace is worth protecting.
Core Beliefs:
Family should always know they are loved.
Patience accomplishes more than anger.
Hospitality creates belonging.
Compassion is strength.
Greatest Fear:
Watching her family become divided.
Greatest Strength:
Compassion, emotional wisdom, patience, and quiet leadership.
Fatal Flaw:
Frequently carries everyone else's worries while ignoring her own.
Psychological Markers:
Calms conflict naturally.
Comforts before criticizing.
Protects traditions.
Sees emotional needs others overlook.
Relationship With Mateo:
Balances Mateo's intensity with warmth and perspective. She understands him better than anyone and often keeps his protective instincts from becoming overbearing.
Relationship With Paloma:
Raises Paloma to value both independence and family, trusting her daughter to choose a man of genuine character.
Locked Thematic Core:
Compassion that preserves family.
Paloma Echevarría Kincaid
Role: Kai's wife • Daughter • Member of the Five Oaks family
Family:
Wife of Kai Weston Kincaid.
Daughter of Mateo and Catalina Echevarría.
Older sister of Isabella Echevarría.
Daughter-in-law of Jake and Krys Kincaid.
Core Spine:
Paloma chooses love without surrendering who she is. She embraces both her Spanish heritage and her new life at Five Oaks, believing that family grows stronger when different traditions are welcomed instead of erased.
Background Foundation:
Raised in a loving Spanish household surrounded by strong family traditions, respect, and deep affection. Learned confidence, kindness, and independence from her parents.
Core Formation:
Love requires trust.
Family can grow without losing its roots.
Kindness creates belonging.
Joy deserves to be shared.
Core Beliefs:
Marriage is a partnership.
Family should welcome rather than exclude.
Traditions deserve to be honored.
Love grows through everyday life.
Greatest Fear:
Losing the closeness of either family she loves.
Greatest Strength:
Warmth, optimism, emotional intelligence, and unwavering devotion.
Fatal Flaw:
Can underestimate how deeply others worry because she naturally expects the best from people.
Psychological Markers:
Naturally affectionate.
Laughs easily.
Curious about others.
Builds relationships quickly.
Adapts without losing her identity.
Relationship With Kai:
Paloma becomes the answer to the future Kai had quietly stopped believing would ever happen. Their relationship is built on friendship, mutual respect, trust, and genuine happiness rather than drama.
Relationship With Krys:
Krys welcomes Paloma almost immediately, treating her like another daughter. Paloma responds with deep affection and quickly becomes part of the heart of Five Oaks.
Locked Thematic Core:
Belonging born from love and acceptance.
Isabella Echevarría 
Role: Younger daughter • Sister • Member of the extended Five Oaks family
Family:
Daughter of Mateo and Catalina Echevarría.
Younger sister of Paloma Echevarría Kincaid.

Isabella approaches the world with curiosity and enthusiasm. She embraces new people and experiences wholeheartedly, believing joy is something meant to be shared rather than guarded.
Background Foundation:
Raised alongside Paloma in a close-knit Spanish family where warmth, celebration, and loyalty were part of everyday life.
Core Formation:
Wonder keeps life beautiful.
Family grows by welcoming others.
Love should be celebrated openly.
Curiosity creates connection.
Core Beliefs:
Every family has something worth learning.
Happiness is meant to be shared.
People deserve a fair chance.
Kindness leaves lasting impressions.
Greatest Fear:
Seeing the people she loves separated by conflict.
Greatest Strength:
Optimism, curiosity, sincerity, and infectious enthusiasm.
Fatal Flaw:
Sometimes trusts too quickly and speaks before considering every consequence.
Psychological Markers:
Quick to smile.
Easily excited.
Romantic at heart.
Enjoys celebrations and family gatherings.
Forms friendships naturally.
Even though she is two years older than Tsula, they form a close relationship that makes Tavi jealous and fear that he is losing his best friend to a girl.
Relationship With Paloma:
Looks up to her older sister while also teasing her with the ease only sisters possess. Paloma remains her closest confidante.
Relationship With Five Oaks:
Immediately falls in love with the warmth and closeness of the Five Oaks family, embracing them as an extension of her own.
Locked Thematic Core:
Joy born from curiosity and connection.
