The Winston Gang:
Jesse ‘Whisper’ Winston  
Eli's immediate boss and primary abuser. He recruited Eli at fifteen by preying on his anger toward Jace. Over the years he became increasingly violent, regularly striking Eli and blaming him for anything that went wrong. He is the man who shoots Krys, and Eli kills him moments later.
James ‘Ironjaw’ Winston  
Whisper's older brother. Eli spends time at Ironjaw's hideout after leaving Whisper. Ironjaw is intimidating and dangerous, but he is noticeably less impulsive than Whisper. Eli leaves from Ironjaw's camp and begins the ride home to Missouri.
Whisper's Regular Men:
Whit  Longtime gang member. He brings information back from town, including the news about Luke's automobile, and is one of the men Whisper trusts.
Bridger  One of the regular outlaws who feeds accusations to Whisper about Eli, often resulting in Eli being beaten.
Hiram  Another member of the gang whose complaints against Eli often lead to Whisper punishing him.
Shorty  One of Whisper's longtime riders. Like Bridger and Hiram, he contributes to Whisper's growing distrust of Eli.
The Jacobs Outfit:
Pearly Jacobs  
A vicious outlaw who later joins forces with Whisper. Eli notes that Whisper never laid a hand on him until Pearly and his men became part of the outfit. Pearly's influence makes the abuse dramatically worse. Eli ultimately kills Pearly while protecting Krys.
Edgar's Outfit
Edgar Post  
Leader of another outlaw group that joins with Whisper to attack Five Oaks after Eli secretly warns his family. Edgar is killed during the failed assault.
Other Named Outlaws
Billy  One of the outlaws associated with the Winston gang before the shooting.
Troy  Another member of the outlaw outfit.
